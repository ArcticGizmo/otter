# Changelog

All notable changes to Otter are recorded here. Otter follows [semantic versioning](https://semver.org).

## 0.1.0

The first polished release — a ground-up visual revamp on top of the original Teams-call → Slack-status core.

- **Dark design system** — a single shared theme drives every surface, with custom-painted toggles and a spinner.
- **First-class settings window** — a resizable dark window with a left navigation rail and pages for Getting started, Slack, Status, and About, replacing the old cramped dialog.
- **Live status preview** — see exactly how your Slack status will look as you type.
- **Polished tray** — a dark, themed right-click menu with a colour-coded status header; left-click the tray icon to open settings.
- **Quiet preference** — turn the call-detected notification on or off from the tray.
- **App icon** — Otter now has a proper otter mark for the taskbar, window, and About page.
